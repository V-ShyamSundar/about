A Multi-Layer Input Approach to Enhance CDSS for Breast Cancer Diagnosis and Treatment

A novel Multi-Layer Input approach to improve Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) for breast cancer. By integrating diverse data types from various sources (the "multi-layer input"), the aim is to provide more comprehensive and accurate support for both diagnosing breast cancer and guiding treatment decisions.
